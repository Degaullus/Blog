Deep fake level 3 and 25 millions stolen

Ok it happended : Deep faking is no longer stucked limited to Lovescam.

In Hong Kong, a meeting took place involving employees, the Chief Financial Officer (CFO), and a clerk. However, it was later discovered that only the clerk was a human participant; all other attendees were AI-generated. The fraudulent meeting utilized past video footage to create the illusion of a real conference. During the meeting, the human clerk was instructed to carry out transactions totaling $25 million. Subsequently, six individuals (this time real), who were the real orchestrators of the fraudulent scheme, were already arrested.
